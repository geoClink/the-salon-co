·WHAT TO EXPECT

An hour, uninterrupted.

Every appointment, regardless of service, follows the same four-part structure. We’ve refined it across thousands of visits.

I

Before — a consultation.

Twenty minutes with your stylist. We talk about your hair, your week, your last cut. Nothing happens until we agree on the plan and the price.

II

During — the room.

Tea or coffee, a warm towel, soft music. You can read, sleep, talk, or work from your phone. There’s no script.

III

Finish — a styling.

Every service ends with a finish — rough-dry, blow-out, or your preferred home style. No upsell, no add-on charge.

IV

After — a schedule.

We book your next appointment before you leave, if you’d like. You can change it any time up to 48 hours before.

·A NOTE ON BOOKING

We book one appointment per stylist per hour. If you need to change yours, we ask for forty-eight hours — so the chair can find a new guest.

A 25% deposit holds your appointment. Cancellations inside 48 hours forfeit the deposit; inside 24 hours, the full service is charged. New guests are asked to arrive ten minutes early for an extended consultation. Walk-ins are welcome for the wait-list only.
