Privacy policy.

Last updated June 2026.

1. What We Collect

When you make a booking through our website, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. We also collect payment information through Stripe — we do not store card details ourselves.

2. How We Use It

We use your information to confirm and manage your appointment, send you a booking confirmation email, and contact you if there is a change to your appointment. We do not use your information for marketing unless you have explicitly opted in.

3. Email Communications

After booking, you will receive a single confirmation email containing your appointment details and a cancellation link. We do not send promotional emails unless separately requested.

4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. The Salon Co. does not receive or store your card number, CVV, or billing address. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to process your payment (Stripe) or deliver transactional email (Resend). Both are used solely for the purpose of completing your booking.

6. Data Retention

We retain booking records for up to two years for accounting and service history purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at info@thesalonco.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics. The only cookies present are those set by Stripe during checkout, which are necessary to complete payment securely.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@thesalonco.com or by calling (555) 555-5555.
