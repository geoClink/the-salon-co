·OUR STORY

Built on the belief
that
hair is a quite
ritual,
not a transaction.

The Salon Co. began with a single chair in a borrowed studio above a bookshop. Seven years later, we are four chairs, three colorists, and one stubborn idea: that the best work happens when no one is in a hurry.

We take fewer appointments than we could. We pour the coffee ourselves. We don't book back-to-back, and we don't double up. Every guest gets the room.

"A haircut is the closest thing most people have to being still for an hour. We try to honor that."

Margaux Hale

·Why The Salon Co.

Slow craft, in a noisy industry

Three principles that govern how we book, how we hire, and what we put on your hair.

I

A studio, not a

Four chairs, soft light, and no walk-ins. Each appointment is the only one in the room.

Education-

Our team trains six weeks a year in London, Tokyo and New York. Technique is the only shortcut we believe in.
