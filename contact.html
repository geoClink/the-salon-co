·Your First Visit

Four small things that’ll
make
the hour a better one.

I.

Arrive ten minutes early.

New guests are booked for an extended consultation. Tea or coffee is on the counter.

II.

Come with photos, if you like.

Or come with nothing at all. We’ll work from your hair, your life, and a long conversation.

III.

Skip the wash if you need to.

We can cut on day-old hair. We can’t cut what’s already wet from the rain.

IV.

Tell us about your week.

How you actually wear your hair on a Tuesday matters more than a Sunday photograph.
