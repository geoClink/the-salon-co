· OUR STORY

Built on the belief
that
hair is a quite ritual,
not a transaction.

The Salon Co. began with a single chair in a borrowed studio
above a bookshop. Seven years later, we are four chairs, three
colorists, and one stubborn idea: that the best work happens
when no one is in a hurry.

We take fewer appointments than we could. We pour the coffee
ourselves. We don't book back-to-back, and we don't double up.
Every guest gets the room.

A haircut is the closest thing most people have to being still for an hour. We try to honor that.

Margaux Hale

FOUNDER & SENIOR STYLIST

· Why The Salon Co.

Slow craft, in a
noisy industry

Three principles that govern how we book, how we hire,
and what we put on your hair.

I

A studio, not
a salon floor.

Four chairs, soft light, and no walk-ins. Each appointment is the only one in the room.

II

Education-forward
stylists.

Our team trains six weeks a year in London, Tokyo and New York. Technique is the only shortcut we believe in.

III

Considered, low-
tox products.

Color and care lines selected for ingredient integrity, scalp health, and provenance. Refill program available.
