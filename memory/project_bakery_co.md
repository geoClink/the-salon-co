The Bakery Co. (the-bakery-co.onrender.com) is a full-stack bakery e-commerce platform — the anchor portfolio project and first vertical of a white-label SaaS platform for local food businesses.

**What's built:**
- Customer storefront: menu, cart, checkout, Stripe payments, loyalty rewards, subscriptions, gift cards, nationwide shipping, account pages, event booking
- Admin dashboard: full CMS for menu, orders, inventory, coupons, customers, revenue reporting, site settings, banner customization, employment applications, daily prep list
- Automated notifications: order confirmation emails/SMS, low stock alerts, birthday coupons, weekly revenue digest, unfulfilled order reminders via node-cron
- Stripe webhook: payments confirmed before orders save, subscription recurring billing
- Anthropic MCP server: customers can place real orders through Claude with Stripe checkout links
- WebMCP (W3C standard): browser-native AI tools on menu, hours, checkout pages for Google Gemini — implemented same week Google announced it at Google I/O
- Jest test suite: menu endpoint and auth protection tests

**Stack:** Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, Node.js/Express, Supabase (Postgres), Stripe, Resend, Twilio

**Business vision:** CH Studios LLC (Detroit) — vertical SaaS, white-label platform for local food businesses. The Salon Co. is the salon vertical. Goal: real business or exceptional portfolio demonstrating production-level thinking.

**Active revenue:** Launched LinkedIn offer building personal brand sites for Apple Dev Academy students — $300 flat, one time, no monthly fees, with client-managed admin dashboard. First clients pending.
